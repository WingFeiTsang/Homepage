➠ About Me

Rongfei Zeng is the associate professor of Software College at Northeastern University and a visiting research fellow of Computer Networks and Information Center, CAS. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University with honor in 2012. Now, his research interests include distributed machine learning and its applications, networks performance and security, cloud computing and IoTs. He has published 30+ papers in top journals and conferences such as IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS), Elsevier Computer Networks, IEEE ICDCS, etc. Currently, he is a senior member of CCF and a member of IEEE and ACM. He also serves as an executive member of CCF TC on Internet, Network & Data Communications, and Distributed Computing, and CCF WC on Science Promotion. He is also the consultant of CCTV2, Posts & Telecom Press, etc.
